<Update label="January 2025" description="">
  ## Multiple choice custom fields
  We've introduced a new type of custom field, called "multiple choice", which allows the shopper to select multiple options from a list of options.

  Find more information in the [Collect Custom Fields](/section/captureData/customFields) section.

  ## Bloomreach Sync Custom Fields
  We've added support for syncing custom fields to Bloomreach. This means you can now sync additional information about your customers to your Bloomreach account, such as their first name, shopping preferences, and more.
  
  ## Shopify App
  We are releasing our brand new Shopify app! Quickly install our script and enable all of our features across your Shopify stores with zero code.

  - **No-code head script installation:** Once you've installed the app and connected your store, we'll automatically install the head script site wide - no dev time required.
  - **Drag and Drop Shopify Blocks:** Simply drag and drop OfferHub, Landing Page and Onsite Messaging blocks onto your pages, no need to edit any code.
  - **Automatic coupon code feed:** Connect your verification flows to your Shopify Discounts and we'll automatically generate unique coupon codes for your customers, so you don't have to.

  Find more information in the [Integrate on Site](/section/gettingStarted/integrateOnSite#shopify) section.
  
  <Info>
    This app is currently in beta and not listed on the Shopify App Store. If you want to try it out, please contact your Customer Success Manager.
  </Info>
</Update>
