<Update label="September 2024" description="">
  ## OSM Visibility rules
    <Frame>
        <img src="/images/changelog/osmVisibilityRule.png" alt="Path rule" width="100%" style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }} />
    </Frame>
    Promote the right offer to the right shopper at the right time. 
    
    Our visibility rules give you complete control over which offers are visible to which shoppers. 
    Find more information in the [On Site Messaging](/section/promoteOffers/osmVisibility) section.
    We currently support:

    - **Time of day:** show an offer between certain hours, catching your shoppers when they're most likely to convert.
    - **Page URL:** show an offer on certain pages of your site, helping you target specific products or categories.
    - **Price:** show an offer for products within a certain price range, ensuring you keep your offers in line with your pricing strategy.
    - **Dwell time:** show an offer after a user has spent a certain amount of time on a page, driving incremental conversions.
    - **Date:** show an offer between certain dates, helping you run targeted campaigns around key dates and events.

    


  
  
</Update>

<Update label="August 2024" description="">
  ## Custom Fields
    <Frame>
        <img src="/images/data-capture/customFields/customfield-overview.png" alt="Custom Fields" />
    </Frame>
    Use [Custom Fields](/section/captureData/customFields) to collect any additional data you require during the [verification flow](/section/captureData/overview). Personalise the shopping experience and retarget shoppers. We currently support:

    - **Free Text:** useful for collecting names, postcodes or other unstructured information.
    - **Dropdown:** best when you have a long list of potential options, such as product categories.
    - **Radio Buttons:** used when you have a short list of potential options, such as product size, and want the user to select one.
    - **Multi Select:** allow users to select multiple options from a list, useful for collecting information such as hobbies or interests.
    - **Date Picker:** make it easy to collect dates, such as dates of birth or other significant events.
