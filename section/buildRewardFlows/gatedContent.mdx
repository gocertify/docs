The Gocertify magic link let's you provide secure access to private content or storefronts with the discounts pre-applied. 


To set up gated content, provide your technical team with a link to this [documentation](/section/buildRewardFlows/gatedContentTechDocumentation).
Your technical team will set up a secure URL that automatically redirects successfully verified shoppers straight to your gated content.
