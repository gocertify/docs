title: 'Reward Guardrails'
description: 'Set limits on how often shoppers can redeem rewards.'
icon: 'timeline'
---

Gocertify makes it easy to set limits on how often shoppers can redeem rewards. This is useful for protecting your rewards from abuse and ensuring they are only redeemed by eligible shoppers.

Set up a limit by asking your Gocertify account manager to enable the feature for your account.
