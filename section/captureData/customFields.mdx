## Overview

By default, enabling DataCapture will collect shopper emails, enabling you to run newsletter campaigns. However, with custom fields you can collect
any additional data you require to personalise the shopping experience and retarget shoppers.

## Custom Field Types
To ensure your data is standardised and easy to use, gocertify supports a range of custom field types. These can be mapped to your own data fields in your CRM.

### Text
Let users input free text; useful for collecting names, postcodes or other unstructured information.
