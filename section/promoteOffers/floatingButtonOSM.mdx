## How to enable floating button placements

You can one click enable floating button placements on any page where you have already installed the Gocertify head script (see [integrate on site](/section/gettingStarted/integrateOnSite)) without the need for a developer.
Simply log into your [Gocertify dashboard](https://secure.gocertify.me/dashboard) and click on the On-Site Messaging tab.

## How to position your floating button placement

You can change the position of the floating button placement using the dropdown menu in the dashboard. For advanced positioning options you can use custom CSS.
