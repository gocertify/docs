Gocertify's promotion engine provides the tools needed to surface the right offer at the right time to the right shopper:
- **[On-site](/section/promoteOffers/on-site-placements) and [In-Store](/section/promoteOffers/inStore):** Deliver omnichannel performance at every shopper touchpoint
- **[High performing Placements](/section/promoteOffers/on-site-placements):** Drive conversions with prominent on-site messaging
- **[Dynamic CTAs](/section/promoteOffers/dynamicText):** Highlight exact savings amounts by passing through product data
- **[Visibility Toolkit](/section/promoteOffers/osmVisibility):** Control exactly when and where offers are surfaced
