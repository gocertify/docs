title: Via Affiliate Network
description: 'Using an affiliate network to track your conversions.'
icon: "fa-fw"
---

If you're already using an affiliate network (like Awin or Rakuten), you can use your existing setup to track verified shoppers through their customer journey. This approach requires minimal technical implementation as the affiliate network handles the attribution.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a new tracking link">
    Create a new tracking link in your affiliate network.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add the tracking link to your campaigns">
    Provide the tracking link to your Gocertify account manager so they can add it to your campaigns.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If you would rather track conversions directly, and understand the incremental impact of your campaigns,you can use our [Direct Conversion Tracking](/section/trackImpact/conversionTracking) feature.
